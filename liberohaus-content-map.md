## Page: Home (`/`)

**Meta title:** Zděné rodinné domy na klíč, levné cihlové stavby – Liberohaus s.r.o.
**Meta description:** Brick family houses built turnkey since 1995; catalog house designs.

### Hero carousel
Rotating banner images of several house models (Trento, Venus, Libero, Pluto) each with a "Detail" link to that house's page, plus a final slide leading into the main intro text.

### Intro section – "Stavíme zděné rodinné domy na klíč" (We build brick family homes turnkey)
Explains that Liberohaus designs and builds low-energy and passive houses, offers a wide choice of heating technology, heat recovery ventilation, and shading, and works with in-house architects/designers so clients can either customize a house or pick a standard design — emphasizing every house is unique. States the price includes a heat pump, full project documentation with site placement, and building-permit handling. Notes the company mainly builds across Moravia, with a Central Bohemia branch, LiberoPrag, offering tailored solutions for that region. Clarifies that the house models shown are illustrative visualizations for inspiration rather than exact repeat builds, and that if a client doesn't pick from the catalog, Liberohaus will design a custom house at no extra cost as part of the construction price, tailored to the plot's orientation and location and the future occupants' needs. Describes the process: based on the client's ideas, the company prepares a design study and price quote before full documentation is completed, so clients learn the cost early. Mentions the company has operated since 1995, accumulating experience in construction technology and methods refined through extensive testing, with a broad portfolio of house designs.

### "Galerie realizací" (Completed Projects Gallery)
A small preview grid of project photos (e.g., a custom bungalow, the "Nova R" house, the "Vito" house) linking to a larger lightbox image, with a "Více realizací" (More projects) link to the full Realizace page.
